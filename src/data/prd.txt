Project Charter: Smart Loan Processing System

1. Project Overview
The Smart Loan Processing System (SLPS) aims to streamline the loan application and approval process for FinTrust Bank by automating document verification, credit scoring, and approval workflows. The system will reduce manual effort, improve turnaround time, and enhance the customer experience through a digital self-service portal.
________________________________________
2. Business Objectives
• Reduce loan approval time from 7 days to 24 hours.
• Minimize manual data entry by at least 60% through automation.
• Increase customer satisfaction (CSAT score) by 25% within six months of launch.
• Improve compliance tracking for all loan applications.
________________________________________
3. Project Scope
In Scope
• Web-based Loan Application Portal for customers.
• Internal Dashboard for Loan Officers and Underwriters.
• Integration with:
  o Credit Bureau API (for credit checks)
  o Document OCR service (for ID and income document verification)
  o Core Banking System (for final approval and disbursement)
• Automated Notifications and Reminders via email and SMS.
• Basic Reporting and Analytics dashboard.
Out of Scope
• Mobile app version (future phase).
• Integration with third-party financial advisors.
• Loan products outside of personal and home loans.

6. Assumptions
• All external APIs (Credit Bureau, OCR) will be available and provide sandbox environments.
• User authentication will leverage FinTrust Bank’s existing single sign-on (SSO) service.
• Regulatory requirements remain stable during project execution.
________________________________________
7. Constraints
• Budget capped at $250,000.
• Delivery timeline: 5 months (Jan–May 2026).
• Must comply with GDPR and local data protection laws.
________________________________________
8. Risks
Risk | Impact | Mitigation
API unavailability from Credit Bureau | High | Implement fallback manual verification process
Delays in document verification model tuning | Medium | Prioritize human-assisted verification initially
User resistance to new system | Medium | Conduct user training and pilot testing
